Quinoa Buddha Bowl

Bowl Prep Time: 20 mins Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup chickpeas, cooked
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups mixed greens
  • 2 tbsp tahini
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook quinoa in water according to package instructions. Let cool slightly.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together tahini, lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  3. Divide the quinoa between two bowls.
  4. Arrange the chickpeas, avocado, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and mixed greens on top of the quinoa.
  5. Drizzle with the tahini dressing.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!

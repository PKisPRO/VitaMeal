Vegetable Biryani

A fragrant and flavorful rice dish layered with spiced vegetables and aromatic herbs.

Prep Time

30 minutes

Cook Time

45 minutes

Servings

4 people

Description

Vegetable Biryani is a classic Indian dish that combines fragrant basmati rice with a variety of spiced vegetables, herbs, and aromatic spices. The layers of rice and vegetables are cooked together to create a flavorful and satisfying meal that's perfect for special occasions or family dinners.

Vegetarian Vegan Gluten-Free High Protein

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, beans, peas, cauliflower)
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 cup mint leaves
  • 1/2 cup coriander leaves
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 cardamom pods
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp biryani masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Few strands of saffron (optional)

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes, then drain.
  2. Heat ghee or oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  3. Add bay leaf, cardamom pods, cinnamon stick, and cumin seeds.
  4. When the spices start to crackle, add onions and saut√© until golden.
  5. Add ginger, garlic, and green chilies, cook for 1 minute.
  6. Add all vegetables and cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Add tomatoes, biryani masala, and turmeric powder.
  8. Cook until tomatoes are soft and oil starts to separate.
  9. Add drained rice and salt, gently mix everything together.
  10. Add 4 cups of water and bring to a boil.
  11. Reduce heat, cover, and cook until rice is done.
  12. Garnish with mint and coriander leaves before serving.

Tips for Perfect Vegetable Biryani

  • Use aged basmati rice for the best aroma and texture.
  • Don't overcook the vegetables to maintain their crunch.
  • Layer the rice and vegetables for better flavor distribution.
  • Let the biryani rest for 10 minutes before serving.
  • Serve with raita or pickle for extra flavor.

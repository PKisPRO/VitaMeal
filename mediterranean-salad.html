Mediterranean Salad

A refreshing and nutritious salad featuring fresh vegetables, olives, feta cheese, and a zesty lemon-herb dressing.

Prep Time

15 minutes

Cook Time

0 minutes

Servings

4 people

Description

This Mediterranean Salad is a perfect blend of fresh vegetables, protein-rich chickpeas, and tangy feta cheese. Topped with a light lemon-herb dressing, it's a healthy and satisfying meal that's ready in minutes.

Vegetarian Gluten-Free High Protein Low Carb

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed salad greens
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • For the dressing:
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine all the vegetables and chickpeas.
  2. Add olives and crumbled feta cheese.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, and oregano.
  4. Season the dressing with salt and pepper.
  5. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  6. Sprinkle with fresh parsley and serve immediately.

Nutritional Information

Calories

220

Protein

8g

Carbs

15g

Fat

14g

Tips for Perfect Mediterranean Salad

  • Use fresh, crisp vegetables for the best texture.
  • Drain and rinse chickpeas well to remove excess sodium.
  • Make the dressing ahead of time to let flavors meld.
  • Add the dressing just before serving to keep the salad crisp.
  • Customize with additional ingredients like artichoke hearts or roasted red peppers.
